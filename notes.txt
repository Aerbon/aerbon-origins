Powers
	Athletic (+2)
	| you have overall better stats. (+2)
	Artificial (-8)
	| You do not naturally regenerate hp (-5)
	| You are immune to many status effects (+1)
	| You cannot eat (-4)
	| You have an Energy meter (+0)
	| Energy is consumed to sate your hunger (+2)
	| You are heavily weakened when your Energy runs out (-2)
	Bioweapon (-1)
	| Villagers fear you. (-1)
	Bloodbath (+2)
	| You have a Slaughter meter, every kill fills the meter. (+0)
	| The higher the meter, the stronger you get. (+2)
	Brick-like
	| You sink like a brick.
	Climber
	| You can climb surfaces. (+2)
	Levels to power (+3)
	| Your stats are increased based on your level. (+3)
	Magical (-4)
	| You cannot eat. (-4)
	| Your XP is drained to sate you. (+3)
	| Iron harms you. (-3)
	Magic-bound (-2)
	| Your XP slowly drains. (-1)
	| Running out of XP kills you. (-2)
	| You spawn with a couple levels. (+1)
	Micro-sized (+3)
	| You are 1/4 normal size. (+1)
	| You take no velocity or fall damage (+2)
	Overheat
	| You have a heat meter (+0)
	| You build up heat as you gain exhaustion (-3)
	| You take damage if you overheat (-3)
	| You can reduce heat by standing still (+1)
	| You can reduce heat by consuming cold items (+2)
	| You gain or lose heat based on the ambient (+0)
	Powered flight (+2)
	| You can flap your wings and gain airspeed. (+3)
	| This causes exhaustion and consumes a flight meter. (-1)
	Repairable (+4)
	| You can heal yourself by consuming iron ingots (+4)
	Weak (-2)
	| Your stats are slightly reduced. (-2)
	Wings (+3)
	| You have elytra when no chestplate is equipped. (+3)
